3. Description of the Leonardogroup@work platform

3.1. General - Scope of the Leonardogroup@work platform

The Leonardogroup@work platform is an online service allowing its Users to be informed of potential risks and/or vulnerabilities in terms of computer security that may affect their computer systems or networks. As specified below, the Leonardogroup@work platform does not perform a vulnerability analysis of Your Network, but only (i) checks the Databases to see if Your IP address or Your Domain appears one or more times, and (ii) informs You if Your IP address Network or Your Domain appears in one or more of these Databases. The Leonardogroup@work platform is available for free.

3.2. Register Your Network and Domain

In order to use the Leonardogroup@work platform, you must authenticate yourself on the Leonardogroup@work online platform. After successful authentication, You will be asked to register Your Network and Your Domain. To ensure that You have the necessary authorization and rights to register the network and domain and receive Alerts for your Network and Domain, You must authenticate yourself.

3.3. Specific Alerts for Your Network and Your Domain

Once you have successfully registered Your Network or Your Domain, the Leonardogroup@work platform registers Your Network or Your Domain and sends it to the backend where your IP address and the infected IP addresses can be compared. If Your IP address matches an infected IP address in the Database, You will be sent a message to Your e-mail address. If your IP address changes, the Leonardogroup@work platform will register your new IP address and send it to the backend.

When does a user receive an alert?

You will receive an Alert if Your IP address appears in one of the Databases and provided that the Leonardogroup@work platform has not previously notified You of the Threat.
You will not receive an alert in the following scenarios:

  • Scenario 1: Your IP address does not appear in any of the Databases.
  • Scenario 2: Your IP address appears in one of the Databases but the Leonardogroup@work  platform has already notified you of the Threat
  • Scenario 3: You have deactivated the Alert reception functionality in the Leonardogroup@work platform.
  • Scenario 4: You have a dynamic IP address and your new IP address is not yet registered in the Leonardogroup@work platform and in the backend. Every time the Leonardogroup@work platform is opened, it checks if your current IP address still matches the registered IP address. If this is not the case, the Leonardogroup@work platform records Your current IP address.
